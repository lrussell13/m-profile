Marissa Merkt is a writer with over seven years of experience in editing and composing on multiple platforms, ranging from magazines to newspapers to websites. Her organizational skills and attention to detail allows her to consistently deliver high-quality work on a tight deadline while juggling multiple tasks.

Marissa holds a bachelor's degree in communications with an emphasis in print and public relations and a minor in sociology. She uses her education and knack for words to engage readers across genres.

When she's not busy writing, Marissa can be found running along the beach or reading beside her calico.
